Why Use WebP?

WebP is a modern image format that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. Using WebP, you can create smaller, richer images that make the web faster.

  • Smaller File Sizes: 26% smaller than PNGs and 25-34% smaller than JPEGs.
  • Faster Loading: Improved page speed scores and SEO rankings.
  • Transparency Support: Full alpha channel support like PNG.

Key Features

1. Visual Comparison Slider

Don't guess the quality. Use our built-in Before/After Slider to visually inspect the difference between your original image and the converted WebP result. Adjust headers, sharpness, and compression artifacts in real-time.

2. Auto-Start Conversion

Efficiency is key. Enable the AUTO_START toggle to immediately process images as soon as you drag and drop them. No extra clicks required.

3. Batch Processing

Upload hundreds of images at once. Our tool processes them in a queue and allows you to download them individually or as a single ZIP Archive.

4. Smart Re-Conversion

Need to tweak a specific image? Change the quality slider and hit the Redo button on any individual item to re-process just that file without re-uploading.

Privacy First: Client-Side Processing

Unlike other online converters that upload your photos to a remote server, our tool runs entirely in your browser.

  • Zero Uploads: Your images never leave your device.
  • 100% Secure: Perfect for sensitive documents or personal photos.
  • Instant Speed: No network latency or upload wait times.

How to Use

  1. Select Images: Drag & drop PNG, JPG, or BMP files into the "Input Stream" zone.
  2. Adjust Quality: Use the slider to set your desired compression level (default is 85%).
  3. Convert: Click INITIALIZE_CONVERSION (or use Auto-Start).
  4. Preview: Click the Eye Icon to compare the result.
  5. Download: Save files individually or click ARCHIVE_ZIP to get everything at once.
